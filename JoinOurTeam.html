Join Our Team

There will be 10 roster spots (male) available that will be filled in [somewhat] traditional tryout fashion. The requirements will be: 2 rounds of minimum of each W-W, B-W and W-B - totaling 6 rounds submitted. The 2 minimum rounds will be averaged together, per layout, then all three will be added together. Lowest 12 cumulative scores will be on roster.

ex: WW: RD 1 - 54 RD 2 - 52 Avg = 53
WB: RD 1 - 59 RD 2 - 65 Avg = 62
BW: RD 1 - 56 RD 2 - 59 Avg = 57.5 Total = 172.50

A player can submit as many rounds as they like, only their lowest 2 scores will count.

Side rules/notes:
- 2 People minimum per card - one must be a Borderland TC player. - 3 People or more on card - if none of the players were on a Borderland TC before. - Not all scores needed to be counted for any given round on any given card. Only those who wish to submit can. - Rounds must be submitted with name, score, layout and who was on card. You can submit a round to either Jon or Tom directly or through the form on thr right. - scores will be rounded to nearest hundredth - Should there be a need for a tie breaker for the last roster spot(s) it will be decided by a 1 round playoff. Layout determined at random on the day of the playoff.
